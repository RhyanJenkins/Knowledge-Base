Tips and Tricks

Easy tip to enter calls in to Service Now:

Keep your Outlook email open next to your ServiceNow window. Once you've sent your follow-up report to maintenance via Lonestar, use the information from your email to copy/paste in to ServiceNow.

You can copy/paste:
Event ID>Reports
Location>General Location
Description Notes>Description

Simply select the line from the email, hold the control key on keyboard, and press C.
Then, click on the correct field box in ServiceNow, hold the control key on keyboard, and press V.

Fill out the rest of the required fields in ServiceNow, and then click on Incoming/Outgoing Call.
