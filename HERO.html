HERO services include:
Relocate disabled vehicles to safety (push or escort.) Remove minor collision from the roadway. Provide traffic and lane control at collision scenes. Remove debris from travel lanes. Change flat tires (if motorist has spare.) Give air to low tires. Provide gasoline (about 1 gallon only.) Provide water to an overheating engine. Perform minor vehicle repairs. Provide jump-start to dead batteries. Assist first responders at collision scenes. Provide drinking water and cell phone services to stranded motorists.
