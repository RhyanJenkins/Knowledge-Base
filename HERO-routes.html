HERO Routes

Routes that are in RED are North Channel 2
Routes in BLUE are South Channel 3
Routes 1, 3, 4, 5, 6 on weekends are Channel 2

On IH-35

  • SH-45 N Toll - Route 5/6
  • SH-45 SE Toll - Route 3

  • Centerpoint Rd - Route 1
  • Center Street (RM-150) - Route 3

  • US-290E - Route 5
  • US-290W/SH-71 - Route 4

  • FM-1626 (near Onion Creek Pkwy) - Route 3
  • FM-1626 (aka Kyle Pkwy) - Route 3

This roadway connects with IH-35 in 2 locations. MAKE SURE YOU DOUBLE CHECK WITH HERO IF HERO ADVISES THIS CROSS STREET.

  • Capital of Texas Hwy - Route 9
