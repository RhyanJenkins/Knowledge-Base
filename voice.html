Checking Voicemail

As of 4/14/2020, all TMC voicemails will now be received via your TxDOT Outlook Email.

The email will come from “ctmavayaalerts@austintexas.gov” and will contain an audio file that contains the voicemail. We need to reply to the email stating the voicemail has been taken care of.

On your desktop computer, make sure your volume is up. Double click on the audio file to open, then press play. You can repeat the message as many times as you need to understand fully. You can use headphones to help, if needed.

On your laptop computer, if you have an addition monitor plugged in, you might not be able to hear audio from your laptop. If this occurs, use headphones to listen to voicemail.

See “CTECC Voicemail Emails” email sent by John Gold on 4/14/2020 at 9:02AM
