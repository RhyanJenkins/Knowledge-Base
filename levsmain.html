HERO vs. Law Enforcement and Maintenance

If Law Enforcement needs to be notified of an event, the following will list which LE agency covers which areas on HERO routes.

If Roy Jorgensen covers an area in addition to another yard, ROY JORGENSEN will be the maintenance yard you call.

Emails must be sent to all yards responsible for area.

Any vehicle within APD jurisdiction will be in APD Wrecker Request area.

Tow request in APD AREA ONLY.
The cross street with an “X” is in the Austin Tow Zone Areas.


See Wrecker Requests tab on how to complete a request. See Google Maintenance map for exact jurisdiction cut-off areas. Any lane blocking events or maintenance events must have these yards added to Lonestar emails.

If not on HERO route, check the maintenance website, maintenance google map, or Maintenance tab above for covered locations.
