Wrecker Request with Austin PD

Impound Wrecker for Abandoned Vehicles:

If HERO requests, and if in APD jurisdiction, send impound tow request email from the Lonestar event to APD Wrecker Request with the following information:



Vehicle color/make/model/plate or VIN info. THE MOST IMPORTANT INFORMATION WILL BE THE MAKE AND THE PLATE/VIN. MAKE SURE YOU HAVE THEM INCLUDED IN THE EMAIL.

“Vehicle is unoccupied. HERO tow requested for impound of abandoned vehicle”

Example:

The reply from APD Teletype will look similar to:

APD will provide the following information:

  • The 9 digit number is called the CAD number
  • The name of the tow company

    • Most common tow companies:

    • Southside
    • Lakeside
    • Austex
  • The name of the approving officer
  • The 4-digit officers badge number (Usually starts with AP)

From example:
200230526 (CAD)
SOUTHSIDE (TOW COMPANY)
SGT MARQUEZ AP 4908 D290 (APPROVING OFFICER AND BADGE NUMBER)

The CAD, approving officer name, badge number, and tow company must be given to HERO ASAP


Service Wrecker for Occupied Vehicles:

If in APD jurisdiction, and motorist agrees, HERO might request a tow for SERVICE, which will pick up the vehicle and bring to nearby auto shop/destination. If so, send an email from the Lonestar event to APD Wrecker Request with following information:


Vehicle color/make/model/plate or VIN info THE MOST IMPORTANT INFORMATION WILL BE THE MAKE AND THE PLATE/VIN
MAKE SURE YOU HAVE THEM INCLUDED IN THE EMAIL.

“Vehicle is occupied. HERO requests tow for service” stated in email.

Example:

The reply from APD Teletype will look similar to:


The only information you NEED to give HERO will be the name of the tow company. They do not need the CAD or officers information.

From example:
LAKESIDE WRECKER ENROUTE ON SERVICE TOW
SIL ACCORD
12000 BLK N MOPAC NB PROPER
193010448
RUSSELL AP4240 A290
(TOW COMPANY)
