Construction Zones

If TMC receives a call about an issue in a construction zone, the contractor needs to be notified.

They need to be notified for:

  • Potholes/roadway issues
  • Construction material has been damaged/moved

To notify the contractor, look through your emails to find the proper contact. If unable to find the contractor in the area, ask your supervisor. If still unknown, call maintenance yard or PIO to see if they are aware of who the contractor is.
